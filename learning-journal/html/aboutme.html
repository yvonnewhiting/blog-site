Hi there! My name is Yvonne and welcome to my learning journal.

I started my learning journey with YouTube tutorials, transitioned to Scrimba, briefly explored Harvard's CS50 course, and now I'm back at Scrimba. Currently, I'm focusing on responsive designs, mastering React and building my portfolio.

Maintaining Commitment to Learning

I consider myself a lifelong learner. In the past, I would dedicate countless hours to acquiring new knowledge and skills. I would then apply what I had learned by creating simple projects or incorporating new techniques into existing ones.

While this approach was enjoyable, I realized that it could be beneficial to share my learning journey with the wider world.

Getting Started

I began this journey by starting small and gradually expanding my learning journal website. I diligently took notes during my learning sessions. After each session, I not only used these notes to reflect on what I had learned but also to write concise summaries in my own words.

This practice not only deepened my understanding of the material but also had the unexpected benefit of helping others on their learning journeys, providing them with motivation and insights.
